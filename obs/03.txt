====== 3. The Flood ======



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-03-01.jpg}}

To pogligad to mgo timpu, nig mon-in on ka mgo otow no nig ugpo kai to kalibutan,
Maroot lagboy si kandan woy mabolot laban.
Lagboy no nigmaroot ka mgo otow, no naka-somamsoman ka Magboboyo no oglogparan din ka kalibutan pinabayo to dakol no lanog.''



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-03-02.jpg}}

Di doun sakboka no otow no natowaron amana to Magbobo-ot kangaran din si Nuwi,maroyow si kandin to tibo no mgo otow, nig-ugpo duma to maroot no mo otow,
woy ka Magbobo-t nig nangon ki Nuwi, 
nakasumansuman-a nog pohingumaan ku ka lanog ka kalibutan,
woy nigkagi ka Magbobo-ot no oghimo ka to dakol lagboy no Arka.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-03-03.jpg}}

Woy ka Magbobo-ot nig nangon ki Nuwi, himu ka to Arka no 140 ka kalayatan, woy 23 ka kalu-agan,woy 13.5 ka katikang. 
Woy nighimu si Nuwi to tatolu no andana, woy duon dogman mgo kuwartu, atop, bintana.
Woy ka soini no Arka ian ka'g luwas to pimilya ni Nuwi woy to tibo no mgo ayam ligkat to dakol no lanug.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-03-04.jpg}}

Woy dutu pigtuman ni Nuwi ka sugu to Magbabayo  duma to kandi no tatolu no anak, woy nohimu ni Nuwi ka pigpohimu to Magbabayo no Arka sulod to Mohon-ing no katuigan, uya su dakol ma amana.
Woy pignangonan ni Nuwi ka mgo otow no duo og hinguma no dakol amana no lanog, woy nikagian ni Nowi ka langun libod kow on to Magbabayo, piru waru nigtuu ka mgo otow kandin.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-03-05.jpg}}

Woy ka Mababayo nig sugu man-on ki Nuwi duma to kandin no pamilia no kinahanglan no og limod kow to mon-ing nog kakuun, woy alog nogkuun to mgo ayam.
To soini no panahon, ka asawa rin woy ka tatolo no anak din no lukos duma to kandan no asawa. insuod on dio to akra-- woy wow-wolo tibo kandan no otow arka



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-03-06.jpg}}

Inpo-ontud to Magbobo-ot duma ki Nuwi dio to baluto ka Boi woy Lukos no mgo ayam woy bayako oyow ogkaluwas kandan to dakol no lanug.
Inpohondio dugman to Magbobo-ot ka papito no lukos woy papito no boi no nigkalkalasi no ayam oyow oggamiton to igpanubad.
To dio on kandan to suud to Baluto lukoban on to Magboboyo the gumawan.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-03-07.jpg}}

Woy nibunsod ka dakol lagboy no oran woy inpouran to Magbabayo solod to 40 no allow,woy to 40 no marusilom, no naabri ka tibo no bayaanan to mgo woig to langit woy to woig, woy ka langun no ingod naponu to woig, woy agad ka malayat no mgo bubungan nalapawan pad.''



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-03-08.jpg}}

Woy ka langun no nangugpo to tano nanamatoy,gawas do to mgo ayam no diot solod to Arka,woy ka Arka nig antow-antow to ampow to woig,woy ka langun no dio to solod naluwas woy waro naapil to lanog.''



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-03-09.jpg}}

Woy to natapus on ka uran,ka Arka nig anuy-anuy to woig solod to lalima no bulan,no to sika no timpu og paganadganad-on og kootihi ka woig,woy to sagboka no allow,ka Arka ni dunggu dio to bubungan,piru ka ingod duon pad man-on woig,woy to pog kaponga man-on to tatolu no bulan ka mgo bubungan ogkakitoon on.''



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-03-10.jpg}}

to napongo-on to 40 no allow, si Nuwi nig sugu man-on to bayaku nog ngaranan to Uwak,no oyow og palo-uyon to woig ko nootian-o soini,woy nig layang on kaso Uwak woy nig pipitow ko duon nagangu no tano, di kaso uwak nig libod no waro naka-alap to duhon.''



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-03-11.jpg}}

Woy nig palayang o man-on si Nuwi to bayaku nog ngaranan to salapati, piru waro  nakita to salapati no nagangu no tano,woy  nig libod man-o ka salapati dio ki Nuwi. Woy pog ligad man-o to saboka no simana,in palayang man-o ni Nuwi ka so salapati. Woy nataman nig libod ka salapati no nig uyan to sugpang to ulibu,woy ka woig nootian o,woy nig libod o man-o nanubu kaso pinamula.''



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-03-12.jpg}}

Woy nig tatahad-o 
man-o si Nuwi to loin no simana,woy in lokaan 
din-o man-o ka salapati,to ikatatolu no timpu nataman dutu  warad-o nig libod ka salapati,woy duon natagan-o Nuwi no nootian-o kas woig.''



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-03-13.jpg}}

To pogkaponga to ika daruwa no bulan,pig nangonan to Magbabayo si Nuwi,koikow woy ka pamilya nu,duma to mgo ayam, 
libwas kow on to Arka!
anak kow to mon-ing woy no hiponu-a nio ka so ingod,
woy dutu niglibwas si Nuwi duma to mo pamilya rin ligkat Arka.''



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-03-14.jpg}}

Woy to nanggawas o si Nuwi to Arka, nighimo kandin to altar woy nig purot si kandin to ayam no oyow ighalad din to Magbabayo. 
Woy ka Magbabayo noholo lagboi to inlahalad ni Nuwi, woy inpanalanginan sikandin to   Magboboyo duma to kandin no pamilya.''



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-03-15.jpg}}

Ka Magbabayo nigkagi, "ogsaad a no kono kud lugparan ka tano tungod to maroot no hinimoan to mo ko-ototawan,
woy kono kud og guguson ka ingod pinabayo to lanug,
agad pad ka mgo otow no makasasalo no ligkat pad to pogkobato dan.''



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-03-16.jpg}}

Woy nig himu ka Magbabayo to hun-a no  baluntu no ig palinou to saad din, 
no ko'og lopow ka baluntu diot ampow to langit, ig palino-u ko nokoy ka in saad din to mgo otaw. Woy ogdoromdomon to Magboboyo ka saad woy kandin no koot-otowan.

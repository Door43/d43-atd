====== 30. Jesus Feeds Five Thousand People ======



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-30-01.jpg}}

Pig sugo ni Hisus ka kandin no mgo hibatoon to pog nangon woy to pog badlong to koototowan, dio to nig koloin-loin lugar. to pog libong dan dio ki Hisus,nig nangon dan ka nohimo ran. woy nig hinggat ni Hisus  ka kandin no mgo hibatoon no og hondio to  holholowan, dio to talipag to lanow oyow nog himoloy si kandan dio. no nig sakoy on si kandan to baluto oyow nog pokohondio to talipag.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-30-02.jpg}}

Piru mohon-in ka mgo otow no nakakita ki Hisus no nig untod to baloto duma to mgo hibatoon din, woy dio namalahoy-on ka mgo otow dio to talipag to lanow oyow nog kohon-an dan si kandan dio. woy to pog lupow ni Hisus dio duma to kandin no mgo hibatoon, mohon-in on ka mgo otow no nig tatahad kandan.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-30-03.jpg}}

Duo 5,000 no mgo lukos dio nalibulong, waro labot ka mgo Boi, woy mgo bato. nohoy-o lagboy si Hisus to mgo otow.oyow pa ni Hisus soino no mgo otow koiling to mgo karniru no waro talagbantoy. woy duo pig tudlo-an on si kandan, woy pig-ayo ka mgo masakiton.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-30-04.jpg}}

To pogkamahapon on, pig nangunan to mgo hibatoon  si HIsus, mahapon-on piru waro pad Ingud no marani kai.pohipanawap pa si kandan oyow tog pamango tog pogkoon dan.''



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-30-05.jpg}}

woy nig nangonan ni Hisus ka kandin no hibatoon, bohayi nio si kandan to pogkoon!.'' woy nig tabak si kandan, munowon noy man to poghimot sikan? su duon do man lalima no pan woy daruwan Isda no doisok.''



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-30-06.jpg}}

Nig nangonan ni Hisus ka kandin no mgo hibatoon,'' papinuuwa no ka mgo otow dio to hilamonon, woy limora no si kandan to tag laliman pulo.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-30-07.jpg}}

Woy pigkuwo ni Hisus ka lalima no pan woy daruwa no Isda,woy nig longag si kandin dio to langit, woy nig pasalamat kandin to pogkoon.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-30-08.jpg}}

Woy pig lotib-lotib ni Hisus ka pan woy ka Isda. woy pigbuhoy rin to kandin no mgo hibatoon, oyow og bohoy to mgo otow. woy sigi pad og pambogoy ka mgo hibatoon to pogkoon piru waro pad ian nuobos,, no ka tibo ian no mgo otow nakakoon ian tibo si kandan



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-30-09.jpg}}

To pogkaponga inlimod to mgo hibatoon, ka nasamo no pogkoon, naka pono pad soini to dusi no bukag!puun do to lalima ka pan woy darowa ka Isda ka pigkoon to tibo no mgo otow.''

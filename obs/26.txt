====== 26. Jesus Starts His Ministry ======



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-26-01.jpg}}

To pog kaponga to pog 0l0g-olog ni Satanas ki Hisus,nig libong si Hisus no naponu to maroson no ispiritu dio to ingod no Galilia,no pig ugpaan din, woy dutu nig panulu si kandin to matag lugar.Woy ka mgo otow nakakagi to maroyow ian sikandin.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-26-02.jpg}}

Woy nighondio si Hisus dio to lungsud to Nazarit,no pig dakolan din.To pog hinguma to allow no tig himoloy,nig hondio si kandin to timplu oyow to pog pnggugud,no nig kuwa si kandin to lalahon ni prupita Isaias oyow nog basohon din,no dutu pig ablian ni Hisus ka linukut no lalahon woy pig pangguhud din to lngun no mo otow.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-26-03.jpg}}

Si Hisus nig basa, ka Ispiritu to Magbobo-ot kai to kodi,pig dihugan-a oyow to pogkagi to mgo otow 'to marodio no guguron to mgo otow no warog kuhimo,to palinggawas to mgo na bako, oyow ka mgo butud og pakakita,woy ka mgo pigdaogdaog og pakagawas on, no nig pohintaga no soin on ka tuig to magbobo-ot, no ug                  luwason din ka kandin no kuututowan.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-26-04.jpg}}

To nigpinuu on si Hisus. ka tibo nig pipitow kandin woy nig parani.woy ka soini no guguron no pigbasa rin mahitungod to misias. si Hisus nig nangon,''ka pig lalag ku kuntoon og katuman on'' pinabayo to narinog nio.''woy ka langun no mo otow noinu-inu,no nig kagi ina-at si kandin ma ka anak Husi?



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-26-05.jpg}}

Naponga nig nangon man-on si Hisus,''Malogot ian kan kagi now
piru og nungonan ku si kanio!waro prupita nog dawaton to kaugalingon din no lungsud.To panahun dongan ni prupita Ilias masulug mo balu diot Israil.No waron uran to tatolun tuig woi tunga,piru waro in pouyan si Elias to Magbabayo oyow og tabang to mgo balu diot Israil,ko kuno dio do ian to balu to lain no lugar.''



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-26-06.jpg}}

Woy nig pabulus si Hisus nig kagi,''To panahun ni prupita Elias,masulug mootow diot Israil no mgo kogangon.piru waro pig ayo ni Elias agad sagbuka kandan, gawas ki Naaman, no igboyag to Israel' no kalaban dan."no ka mgo otow no naminog ki Hisus no dio dogman ka mgo Hudio.no naka rinog to pigkagi rin nabulo si kandan.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-26-07.jpg}}

The people of Nazareth dragged Jesus out of the place of worship and brought him to the edge of a cliff to throw him off of it in order to kill him. But Jesus walked through the crowd and left the town of Nazareth.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-26-08.jpg}}

Then Jesus went throughout the region of Galilee, and large crowds came to him. They brought many people who were sick or handicapped, including those who could not see, walk, hear, or speak, and Jesus healed them.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-26-09.jpg}}

Many people who had demons in them were brought to Jesus. At Jesus’ command, the demons came out of the people, and often shouted, “You are the Son of God!” The crowds of people were amazed and worshiped God.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-26-10.jpg}}

Then Jesus chose twelve men whom he called his apostles. The apostles traveled with Jesus and learned from him.

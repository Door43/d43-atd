====== 22. The Birth of John ======



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-22-01.jpg}}

To pog ligad,ka Magbabayo nakig lalag to mgo otow pinabayo to Anghil,woy to mgo Prupita.Piru topog 400 no tuig,warad-o nakig lalag to kandan.to sahuhuno ka Anghil nig hondio ki sa Zacarias  woy to asawa rin no si Elisabit, oyow og nangon to ig panangon to Manama kandan,woy ka soini no otow amana no matinumanon to Magbabayo,piru kono si kandan og paka anak.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-22-02.jpg}}

Ka anghil nig nangon ki Zakarias,woy to kandin no asawa; og paka anak kow to lukos,woy hingarani no si kandin ki Juan. Napono si kandin to maruson no panisingan, woy si kandin ka og pohitaga to mo otow no duo og hinguma no Misiyas!''nig tabak si Zakarias,buyag ad-o woy ka asawa ku,monuwon noy naan pad tog pakaanak!monuwon ku tog pakataga no sika og hinguma?''



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-22-03.jpg}}

Nig tabak ka Anghil ki Zakarias,pig suhu-a to Manama no oyow og uyan to maroyow no guhuron.Uya su kono kama og tuu kodi,puun kuntoo kono ka og paka lalag taman to og losut kas bato.''Puun duo,warad o nakalalag si Zakarias.Woy nig ongkoran to Anghil si Zakarias.No pog uli ni Zakarias to baloy ran ka asawa rin naboros on.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-22-04.jpg}}

To hon-om-o no bulan ka gotok ni Elisabit,nig sahuhuno nig pakita man-o ka anghil to sulod ni Elisabit no og nagaranan ki Maria.Sabokan malitan no ulay pad tantu'woy ian ka og asawoon to otow nog hingaranan ki Husi.Nig nangon ka Anghil,''Og kaboros ka!woy og anak ka to lukos no hingaranni nu si kandin to Hisus.no ian ka anak to Manama no warog poko-iling,woy si kandin ka og dumala to warog katamanan.''



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-22-05.jpg}}

Si Maria nig tabak,''may naa tog kaboros-a to ulay-a man pad?''
ka Anghil nig pasabut ki Maria,''puun to maroson no panisingan kaig kaboros nu,woy gahum to Manama ka og bohoy  koykow to kalayag.ka sika no bato amana no maroson,woy anak to Manama.''No dutu nig palintutuu si Maria,woy pig dawat din kan nangon to Anghil.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-22-06.jpg}}

To naponga nig pakiglalag ka Anghil ki Maria,si Maria nig hondio ki Elizabit oyow to pog Bisita,to pogkarinog ni Elizabit no nig bisita kandin si Maria,ka bato no dio to gotok ni Elizabit nig lotu woy noholo.Woy dutu ka mo malitan noholo to pig himu to Manama kandan.To naponga si Maria non bisita ki Elizabit solod to tatolu no Bulan, nig uli man-o si kandin to baloy rin.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-22-07.jpg}}

After Elizabeth gave birth to her baby boy, Zechariah and Elizabeth named the baby John, as the angel had commanded. Then God allowed Zechariah to speak again. Zechariah said, “Praise God, because he has remembered his people! You, my son, will be called the prophet of the Most High God who will tell the people how they can receive forgiveness for their sins!”

====== 18. The Divided Kingdom ======



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-18-01.jpg}}

To pog ligad to malayat no mo kapananahunan,to namatoy-o si David,woy ka anak din no si sulumun iyan kan puli no nig Hari to Israil.Woy nakig lalag ka Manama ki sulumun woy pig inso din ko nokoy ka butang no igkoupii rin no igbohoy.Nig buyu si kandin to kaalam,woy noholo amana ka Manama to pig buyu ni Sulumun woy pig himu rin si kandin no pinakamaalamon to langun no ingod.Masulug amana kas notounan din woy'g kato-u si kandin no og panulu,woy pig padatu amana to Manama si kandin.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-18-02.jpg}}

To sika,nig himu si Sulumun to Timplu dio to Jirusalim no ig himuwan porom to amoy rin.Dio dan pigsimba ka Manama woy nig panubad,woy warad-o dio to tulda no tomuwanan,woy ligkat to sooyo,Duon o ka panisingan to Manama to timplu no nig ugpo woy nig duma to langun no mgo koot-otawan.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-18-03.jpg}}

Piru noygugma si Sulumun to mo Malitan dio to loin no mgo ingod.Pig supak din ka Manama uya su nig asawa si kandin to hapit og hinguma to 1,00o no  malitan!Masulug to mgo asawa rin no nig puun to loin no ingod woy nig uyan to mgo dios-dios dan no pig pabulusan dan ton simba,Woy nig simboo dogma si Sulumun to mgo dios-dios dan to  sooyo to timpu no nabuyag-os kandin.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-18-04.jpg}}

Woy nabolu-o ka Manama ki Sulumun uya su waro man-o nig simba to kandin.Woy dutu pig tonga din-o to daruwa ka ginhari-an to Israil.to timpu no namatoy o si Sulumun.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-18-05.jpg}}

To pog kamatoy o ni Sulumun,ian man-o nohimu no hari ka anak din no si Rehuboam,saboka si kandin no og koungol-ungol no otow.Nighibulung ka langun no mo otow to Israil woy pighimu ran no hari si Rehuboam,woy nig reklamu si kandan ki Rehubom to mabogat amana no og himuwon,woy dakol no buhis no pig buyu ni sulumun kandan.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-18-06.jpg}}

No mig tabak si Rehuboam no waro nig sumansuman,''Nan niyu ubag no pig lisud-lisud kow-o to kodi no amoy?og timulan kupad man-o ka og himuwon niyo,woy mas subla pad ka ig logpad ku kaniyu.''



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-18-07.jpg}}

Sapulu no tribu to ingod to Israil ka og ogot ki Rehuboam,woy   daruwa ro no tribu kanig tuman kandin.woy ka soini so daruwa no tribu ian ka ingod no Juda.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-18-08.jpg}}

Ka sapulu no tribu to Israil,nig ogot ki Rehuboam, woy nig alam si kandin to otow no ngaranan ki  Jeroboam.Dio si kandan to
 igkagibang no balabahan dapit te igsile...no mig pos-ok to gingharian no ian ka pig umow no gingharian to Israil.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-18-09.jpg}}

Nig supak si Jeroboam to Manama,woy dutu'n puun nanakasalo-o ka mgo otow.Nighimu sikandin to dios-dios no oyow og simbohon to kandin no mgo koot-otawan,woy waro dan-o simbaha kas Manama no dio to Simbahan to ginhari-an to Juda.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-18-10.jpg}}

Woy ka ingod to Juda, woy to Israil,nohimu si kandan no pog og-ogotoy,woy malasi on si kandan og kagubut.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-18-11.jpg}}

In the new kingdom of Israel, all the kings were evil. Many of these kings were killed by other Israelites who wanted to become king in their place.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-18-12.jpg}}

All of the kings and most of the people of the kingdom of Israel worshiped idols. Their idol worship often included sexual immorality and sometimes even child sacrifice.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-18-13.jpg}}

Some of the kings even sacrificed their children to false gods. Most of the people of Judah also rebelled against God and worshiped other gods.

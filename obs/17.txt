====== 17. God’s Covenant with David ======



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-17-01.jpg}}

Si Saul ka hun-a no hari to Israil.Malayat si kandin woy maroyow no otow,no ian  ka ig koupii to mgo otow.Maroyow ka pog alap din to Israil to pipila no mgo katuigan,piru to nalugoy noymu si kandin no maroot woy waro din tumana kas Magbabayo,woy ikan-naa nig pili ka Manama to lain no otow no igpahari din to ingod no ig liwan ki Saul.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-17-02.jpg}}

Si David sagboka no batan-on  to Isralita,no ian kan pili to Manama  no hari no ig liwan ki Saul.Talagbantoy si kandin to karniru woy dio nig ugpo si kandin to Lungsud to Bitlihim.ko duo mo liyun woy usu no og koon to mgo karniru to kandin no amoy no in bantayan din,og panghimatayan din.                mapa-inubsanon si kandin no otow,maroyow no nig tuo woy nig tuman to Magbabayo.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-17-03.jpg}}

To waro pad nohimu no bantugan no Sundalu woy igbuyag si David,nakalaban din pad kas dakol no otow nog hingaranan ki Gulayat,to timpu no bato pad si kandin,Bantugan no Sundalu si Gulayat woy makosog lagboy woy tatolu no mitrus ka kandin no kalayatan.Piru pig tabangan to Manama si David no og koymatayan din si Gulayat,woy og kaluwas din ka Israil.Woy masulug ka pig pildi rin    no mgo kalaban to Israel,
woy mohon-in ka kadaugan din woy
 ,tongod to soini no noymu rin pig sayo, si kandin to mgo otow.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-17-04.jpg}}

Sikan-naa nasina si Saul ki david uya su noupian ka mgo otow kandin,woy pig ol-ologan din si David ton himatoy to ka mon-in.Ikan naa nig holos-holos-o si David kandin.Saboka no allow,
pig pamitow rin si David su oyow og himatayan din,woy nig solod sikandin to Langub no in holosan  ni David.Piru waro nakataha si Saul no dio bos nig holos si David.To sooyo, to marani on si Dabid ki Saul wa din on soini himatayi,hinuun pig gisi-an din do ian to pakabo si Saul oyow ig poila no waro sumansuman din no og puliyan si kandin to pog ka hari din.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-17-05.jpg}}

Eventually, Saul died in battle, and David became king of Israel. He was a good king, and the people loved him. God blessed David and made him successful. David fought many battles and God helped him defeat Israel’s enemies. David conquered Jerusalem and made it his capital city. During David’s reign, Israel became powerful and wealthy.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-17-06.jpg}}

Woy ig koupii ni David no og pos-ok si kandin to simbahan  to tibo no mgo Isralita oyow og simbahan woy og halaran dan to Manama ran,su 400 o no tuig no dio do iya og simba woy og panubad to tulda no tomuwanan no pig himu ni Mowisis.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-17-07.jpg}}

Piru nig pouyan ka Manama to prupita nog ngaranan ki Natan woy pig palalagan din si David,                           ''Manggugubat ka,ikannaa konon si koikow ka og himu to simbahan para kodi.Ka koikown anak ka og pos-ok to sika.Piru og panalanginan kus koikow. Woy saboka                                                                                                                                                                                                       to koikow no mgo kaliwat no ka oghari to kodi no mgo koot-otawan to waro katamanan. ''Ka mesiyas ian ka kaliwat ni David no og hari to warog katamanan,su pinili si  kandin to Manama,woy ian kag luwas to mgo otow ligkat to kandan no mgo salo.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-17-08.jpg}}

To pogkadinog ni David to soini,nig pasalamat ian si kandin woy in sayo din ka Magababayo tongod to kandin no saad woy dakol no koholo-an,woy masulug no mgo panalangin.Waro nakataga si kandin ko kon-u-a og tumana to Manama ka saad din.Woy nig tatagad pad ka mgo Isralita to mgo 1,000 pad tuig hundu-on nig hinguma ka so Mesiyas.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-17-09.jpg}}

Nig hari si David no makatarunganon woy matinumanon to Manama to pipila no katuigan ikan-naa pig panalanginan si kandin.Piru to  mowri nig pakasalo dod amana si kandin to Manama.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-17-10.jpg}}

To saboka no allow nig hiphipanow si David to kandin no palasyo woy nakita rin ka sabo ka no malitan nog ngaranan ki Batsiba.to sooyo no timpu waro ka mgo hibatoon din su nakiggubat to mariyu.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-17-11.jpg}}

Woy nig pitowpitow si kandin to kilid.no naponga pig pakuwa si kandin ni David woy in huliran din to naponga pig poulidin dio to baloy ran ka malitan.To waro malugoy pig nangonan ni Batsiba si David no naboros-o si kandin.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-17-12.jpg}}

Ka iglukos ni batsiba no si uria,saboka no maroyow to mgo sundalu ni David.pig popouli ni David si Uria su oyow og pohondiyaan ka igbio rin .Piru waro nig sugut si Uria noog uli to baloy
su og katiglingoy to dumaran no nakig gubat pad.Woy pig palibod ni David si kandin Woy pig nangonan din kas igbuyag tomo sundalu no igpatangko si Uria to pinakamabulut no mgo kalaban su oyow naa nog kamatoy si kandin.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-17-13.jpg}}

To namatoy on si Uria.Pig asawoo ni David si batsiba woy nanganak si kandin to lukos.Nabolu amana ka Manama to pig himu ni David,woy pig palalagan to Manama ki prupita natan moitongod to kandin no salo.Nighinulsul si David to Manama woy pig pasaylu si kandin to Manama,puun dutu nig matinumanon-os kandin to Magbabayo,woy agad to kalisod to timpu.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-17-14.jpg}}

Piru namatoy ka anak ni David no lukos su piglogparan sikandin  to Manama tongod to kandin no salo,woy'n pog un-unayoy pog-ogotoy ka pamilya rin,nataman ton namatoy-o si kandin.Tongud to sika nig mohuyo ka kandin no pog hari,uya so waro nig tuman si kandin to Magbabayo,piru in tuman dod ian to Manama kain saad din kandin. Woy to nalugoy si David woy to asawa rin no si Batsiba naka anak dod to lukos woy pig ngaranan dan ki sulumun..
